Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 6, 2026

Full Snack Devs LLC ("we," "our," or "us") operates mobile applications published on the Apple App Store. This Privacy Policy describes how we handle information in connection with our applications.

Covered Applications

This policy applies to all applications developed and published by Full Snack Devs LLC, including:

  • Pixel Fury
  • Ninja Focus
  • Galaxy Glide
  • Balloons: Skyward Quest

Information We Collect

Our applications do not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal information or user data. Specifically:

  • We do not collect names, email addresses, or contact information through our apps.
  • We do not collect location data.
  • We do not use analytics, tracking, or advertising SDKs.
  • We do not use cookies or similar tracking technologies.
  • We do not collect device identifiers or usage data.
  • We do not require user accounts or sign-in.

Third-Party Services

Our applications do not integrate with third-party analytics, advertising, or data collection services. No data is shared with third parties.

In-App Purchases

If any of our applications offer in-app purchases, these transactions are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple's handling of payment data is governed by Apple's Privacy Policy.

Children's Privacy

Our applications do not collect personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13. Our apps are safe for use by all age groups.

Data Retention

Since we do not collect any user data, there is no data to retain or delete. Any game progress, settings, or preferences are stored locally on your device and are not accessible to us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. We encourage you to review this policy periodically.

Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data, there is no personal information to access, correct, or delete. If you have questions about your data in relation to our apps, you are welcome to contact us.
